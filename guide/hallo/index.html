工作环境

参考:如何自在的折腾QPy

所以, 整体上:

    [[Android in mobile]]
        +- /etc/profile -> ln -s
        |                  V
        |                  V
        |    /storage/sdcard0/com.hipipal.qpyplus/qpy_profil
        |                  ^
        |                  ^ gen. the 
        |                  ^
        +- QPython -> gen_qpy_env.py
        |    .../projects/MyProject
        |             ^
        |             ^
        `- SSHDriod + id_rsa.pub
                  ^
                  ^   scp and run
                  ^
            /-> base fabfile.py
            |
        +- Fabric
        +- ...
        +- Python
        |
    [[coding at local]]

    all in same wifi domain

fabric

$ fab ?
...

Available commands:

    env       echo Android sys. env
    envgen    gen. into: /storage/sdcard0/com.hipipal.qpyplus/projects/qpy_profile (must link as /etc/profile at first)
    pumod     pumod         scp -r mod up QPy projects/(foucs "3party")
    push      push:proj=chaos   scp all *.py up QPy projects/(default "chaos")
    qpy       qpy:main=hello.py develop main loop:auto do all for QPy(default proj. as "chaos", change by `fab push:proj=*`)
    qpy_call  qpy_call:main=MY.py   (default is "main.py")
    uname     echo Android sys. info.
